DOOM Mapping

I started making maps for DOOM II in December of 2019, looking for a convenient way to start dabbling in 3D level design. I have a lot of nostalgia for DOOM too having installed it on all of the school PCs that I used when I was a student. You will need a copy of DOOM2.wad and a source port to be able to play my levels.
