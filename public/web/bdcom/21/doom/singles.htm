I created this map to teach myself the basics of DECORATE and UDMF. It is a remake of the very beginning of my first proper map MALAGARD. Initially I was going to remake the entirity of MALAGARD in this style but ran into some issues. I designed this map to be a humungous space to explore but it quickly became too big and the node builder began to express unusual buggy sector behaviour and I had to cut the map short. I'm not sure the modifications I made to the player and enemies have entirely worked out but the map remains an interesting and unique experience.

MALAGARD

I've been making maps for 2D games for a while but I thought it was time to expand into Doom. I started learning how to use doom builder late October and am proud to release my first map! This is a 45-50 minute map that only works in GZDoom as far as I know. Uses stock Doom II textures. Difficulty levels available. Jump and crouch disabled. Mouselook optional. Save points are built in to the map so making your own saves is cheating.

I tried to keep this map very classic in style, so no slopes or dynamic lights etc were used. I did have to use 3d floors for ceilings in places but that was more due to being naive about sectors and how the sky worked. If I were to remake this I would probably include more void sectors to improve performance. That being said, one of my favourite things about the map is the long lines of sight and being able to see parts of the map that are coming up next. I'm looking forward to making more maps in the future. I had a lot of fun making this so I hope you enjoy playing and let me know what you think!

Special thanks to Bridgeburner's The Hellforge for answering my questions and providing a place to hang out. Thanks to the tutorials on DragonFly's website. Thanks to Major Arlene for playtesting.

Dire Flu
Hellforge Speedmapping Session 9 MAP17

Theme: Projectile hell (only projectile throwing monsters) and Weapon slot 9 - Barrels.

I based the theme of this map on the fireblu texture dividing it into two sides, one red and one blue. Starting in the blue side, you will face imps, hell knighs, and cacodemons with your shotgun and chaingun. After passing through the white and fireblu portal room and getting your plasma rifle and rocket launcher, you will enter the red side which is filled with barons, arachnotrons, revenants and cyberdemons.
