waNder... for NReality

Modded image maps for NReality.

waNder... is an image-map adventure pack utilizing the modifications of NReality. Gain new abilities as you progress through the pack.

Check out the maps on NUMA or download the individual episodes packaged with NReality, ready to play, below.

Unfortunately, image maps NO LONGER PROPERLY WORK in NReality. I think it has something to do with the death of Adobe Flash Player but I can't be sure. The maps are still available to play but the foregrounds will not show. This unfortunate issue means that waNder... is now cancelled.
