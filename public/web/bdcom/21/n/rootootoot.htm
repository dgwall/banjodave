rootootoot

Mappack for N v1.4 by Metanet Software

I compiled a list of every map I've ever made and whittled it down to my favourite 500. The maps were then organized into episodic format, with 10 columns of 10 episodes, 5 levels per episode, sorted in order of difficulty and complexity. Included in the pack is an even shorter shortlist of 50 of the 500 maps compiled in a single column - column r. Also included are 50 remixed bonus maps in column b. Column b features 10 maps of each of the following types of remix: RED GOLD, HARD MODE, TOPSY TURVY, CHAOS EDITION, and DATA CORRUPTION.
