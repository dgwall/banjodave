Wired Steel

In 2016 I attempted to create a game with movement mechanics similar to that of Super Smash Bros. Melee. I successfully created a boxy robot character maneuverable with an xbox controller with nuanced mechanics of movement.

Features

I programmed the demo with the following features:

  • Procedural damage model
  • Randomized characters
  • Jump squat and double-jump
  • Ledge grabbing
  • Directional air dodging
  • Wavedashing
  • Dash dancing
  • Shield, lightshield, powershield, and shield roll
  • Tumble fall, teching, and tech-rolls

In the end, the code became rather messy and I resolved to refactor it into a more manageable structure, but I never quite got around to it. I have still not fully abandoned the Wired Steel idea however, and the little robot may just re-appear in a future platformer that I have been planning.
