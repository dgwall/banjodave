Game Jams

While I don't enter as many as I perhaps should, I sometimes enjoy the challenge of a game jam: creating a game in just a few days.

Fall of the 20 Pillars

For the GameMaker 20 Jam, I got the beginnings of a top down shooter made. The player controlled ship has destructible wings which, if destroyed, make the ship harder to control. I only spent a couple hours on this and didn't get too much done for this jam as I spent the whole first day making DOOM maps instead. Video is available above.

COLLAT

This was for GMTKJam 2019. The theme was "Just One". I made a little keyboard and mouse shooter. Move your little guy around the screen, pick up the gun with only one bullet, and clear the screen of enemies. The game can be downloaded from itch.io.

birdybirdgame

A short platformer made for a friend's Discord server. Your character has multiple jumps. There are 100 nuggies to collect. Four levels and a final boss. Video above or download here [birdybirdgame_v1.exe 12.9 MB].

eeevvooo

Made for the first Game Maker's Toolkit Jam in 2017. The theme was "Downwell's Dual Purpose Design". In this platformer I created a throwing spear which acts as a weapon but also can be utilised to aid movement. A thrown spear can be bounced off of to gain height. The timing of this jam coincided with a viewing party for The 2017 Evolution Championship Series so I didn't get as much done as I'd hoped. The music was made by my friend Sheezy. Video above. The game can be downloaded from itch.io.
