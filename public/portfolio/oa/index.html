About Us


Image credit: Carol Smith In Focus Daily

Welcome to Outback Adventures, your guide to Australia's most stunning natural landscapes. We believe that responsible tourism is the key to preserving our planet's beauty for future generations, and we are committed to sustainability in everything we do. Whether you're a seasoned adventurer or just starting out, we have the perfect tour for you.

We offer a range of guided tours for adventure seekers of all levels, including multi-day hiking trips, camping expeditions, and wildlife safaris. We also offer customized tours for small groups or families, as well as team-building and leadership development programs for businesses and organizations. All tours are led by experienced guides who are passionate about Australia's natural beauty and culture, and who prioritize safety and environmental responsibility.

Tours and Activities

Our tours and activities are designed to give you an authentic, immersive experience of Australia's natural beauty and culture. From multi-day hiking trips in the Outback to wildlife safaris in Far North Queensland, we have something for everyone. All of our tours are led by experienced guides who are passionate about sharing their knowledge and love of Australia's landscapes.

At Outback Adventures, we are committed to sustainable tourism practices that protect the environment and support local communities. We partner with local conservation organizations to promote biodiversity and protect endangered species.
